Welcome. I'm **Ehsan** — a senior full-stack engineer with a heavy backend lean, based in Kaiserslautern, Germany.

This site has two purposes:

1. **A portfolio.** A clean, honest look at the projects I've built and the experience behind them.
2. **A notebook.** A place where I write down what I've learned — about backend engineering, system design, testing, and the rapidly moving world of AI-assisted development.

## What you'll find here

- **Project case studies.** Not just screenshots — the decisions behind each system, what worked, what I'd do differently.
- **Engineering notes.** Short, focused posts on specific topics: a query I tuned, a pattern I tried, a tool I evaluated.
- **AI in the loop.** I'm an early adopter of Cursor, Claude Code, and MCP servers. Expect notes on what's worth using and what's just hype.

## Why a personal site at all?

GitHub READMEs are a great start, but they don't connect the dots. A portfolio lets me show *how* I think about a problem, not just the code that came out of it. If you're considering working with me, this is the closest thing to a conversation we can have asynchronously.

— Ehsan
