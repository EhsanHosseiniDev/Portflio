## Overview

Designed an IoT-based system that transmits real-time data from smart power meters to centralized servers over raw TCP/IP. The architecture splits ingestion, preprocessing, and delivery into independent workers so the pipeline can absorb traffic spikes without coupling components.

## Architecture

- **Ingestion gateways** terminate TCP connections from meters and shovel raw frames into a durable queue.
- **Preprocessing workers** decode the proprietary frame format, enrich with metadata, and emit normalized events.
- **Event delivery** pushes events to downstream consumers (dashboards, billing, alerting) over an event bus — late subscribers replay from the persisted stream.
- **Backpressure-safe**: the slowest consumer never blocks ingestion.

## Results

The system holds up under sustained high message rates. The clean separation of concerns meant new consumers (a fraud-detection workload, a per-region dashboard) could be added without touching ingestion code.
